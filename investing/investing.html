Financial Literacy

I have been investing for 27 years now, but I would not consider myself an expert. I always consider myself a student of Savings and Investing. I am always learning and taking courses on the topics of Financial Planning and Investing. This is not designed to be financial advice as I am not a professional. This is designed to help you learn from my experiences. The one thing we do not teach in our educational system is Financial Literacy. This is one topic we are forced to go out and learn on our own. Statistics show that the average American only has about $150,000 saved for retirement. That leaves many Americans unprepared for retirement or any other major life event. When I got into learning about Personal Finance and Investing, it dramatically changed my life. In recent years, I have learned more and more about financial planning through courses on these topics. Here I am going to share with you my understanding of the concepts so they might help you develop an interest in learning to Save and Invest. That way you can decide if it's something you want to pursue more before spending money on any courses. Hopefully, I have covered these concepts well enough that you won't even have to spend money on learning more.
