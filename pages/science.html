Learn The Science

This section is about the science behind the companies. I love learning about science, and I love teaching others about science. This section will include everything educational about topics like Hematology, Immunology, Genetics and Oncology. I plan to keep adding new science topics over time. This is a learning resource. It is not necessary to know the science to be a biotechnology investor, but it really helps. I find that scientists, doctors, students and patients tend to like this section the most. Just a word of caution as this is not designed to be medical advice. You should always refer to your doctor.
