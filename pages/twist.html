Twist Bioscience

Profile

Twist is the DNA company. They have multiple platforms around DNA technologies. They have a business around DNA synthesis for the synthetic biology space. This business makes and sells DNA for biotech applications. They have a second business for DNA sequencing kits. Their third business is around designing antibodies for drug discovery companies. Their last business is for DNA as a storage system for data. They currently have sales for the synthetic biology and kits businesses. The rest is being developed for potential future growth.

Management

I have only been following Twist for about a year. I think Emily Leproust is doing a great job at running the company. She seems to do a great job balancing the short term performance of sales with investing in the future with new areas of development. They have two commercial segments that earn revenues while driving growth. They are investing their cash into building out new areas around biopharma and DNA storage.

Science

Their key technology is DNA synthesis on a chip. They are capable of packing a million oligo pools onto a single chip that can synthesize DNA. They reduce the amount of nucleotides used by 98% which saves on cost. They sell DNA made by their synthesis business where anyone can submit a strand of DNA code, and they will synthesize it and ship it to order. It is a great business with them doing DNA for the Synthetic Biology space and Ginkgo Bioworks.

They have a second business in the Next Gen Sequencing space by developing various testing kits. They have various different kits they offer for sale which offer higher efficiency than other kits in this space. Their kits are 50% more efficient for the required amount of sequencing needed. This is another business that does well.

They have a third business around developing antibodies for biotech companies. This is a very early stage business and it takes time for these programs to go through development and reach commercial use. They use their expertise around DNA to help design these antibody therapies. They get milestones and royalties on these programs.

Their last business is around developing DNA for storage of information. This can only be profitable if they can reach the level of storage that makes it profitable. That makes this a challenging and risky area of expansion as we don't know if they will ever be able to reach that level. They plan to have an initial product to test by the end of this year.

Valuation

Cash $438 million

They currently have $265 million in estimated 2023 sales. Even with a low 5 multiple on those sales, that would be worth $1.325 billion in valuation.

They have an entire pipeline of partners in the antibodies development space. It is really hard to value this pipeline right now until we know more about the programs and the data they achieve. We don't know what kind of milestones or royalties they get on these programs. This could be a huge driver of value in the future. I would give this segment a valuation of $250 million value.

All in, that is a $2.013 billion market cap. Based on the 56.858 million shares outstanding , that comes to $35.40.

Events

They continue to assemble their first end-to-end DNA data storage system in anticipation of pilot production to support early access to their Century Archive product toward the end of the calendar year.
