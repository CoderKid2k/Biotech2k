Invitae

Profile

Invitae is a company that does genetic testing using kits. They test for things like Cancer Genetics, Rare Genetic Disease and Women's Healthy. They generate over 3 million samples per year and continue to grow. This makes them like the Google of Genomics information. They allow patient to share their information which can lead to patients getting matched with doctors or scientists who are doing clinical trials that might apply to them. This can provide powerful population level genetics to scientists and researchers alike who are doing research on these diseases.

Pipeline

They have multiple Tests for Different Genomics applications in Clinical Development.

Science

The Oncology space of genetic test is expected to grow to over $30 billion a year market size. There is a huge genetics market for oncology as ever tumor for every patient is unique. There is a need for multiple tests per patient throughout their course of treatement. There is a high level of relpase rates in cancer which will drive continued testing. The more we understand oncology the more important the need for genetics profiling of tumors becomes. This space will only continue to grow as personalized cancer treatments become more popular. One of the major current drivers of oncology is the Minimal Residual Detection (MRD) of cancer.

Its is estiamted that the Women's health space is about $2.5 billion market. Its in not the reason I would own this company or any other testing company. The key focus in on oncology.

The rare Disease market is one of the primary needs for genetic testing, but its not even remotely as big as the oncology market.

Valuation

Cash $550 million

They have reached a run rate of $516 million a year in sales with strong ongoing growth. At 5x sales for a typical price to sales for biotech companies, that comes to $2.5 bilion in valuation based on current sales.

They have about $1.7 billion in debt. That is a negative to the valuation and has to be deducted.

All in, that is a $1.35 billion market cap. Based on the 243 millon shares outstanding, that comes to $5.55.

Events

They are developing new tests for different indications.
