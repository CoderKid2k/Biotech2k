Scribe Therapeutics

Profile

Scribe is working on its own unique version of CRISPR called CASX. The orignal CAS9 system was discovered in Step bacteria. This led other companies to go out and look for other forms of CRISPR in other organisms like bacteria and archea. CASX was discovered from Ecoli bacteria. CASX works very much like CAS12 with staggered doubles stranded breaks. It is smaller than the original CAS enzymes at about 1,000 amino acids in length. It even uses a TTCN PAM sequence. This gives us a whole new CAS enzyme for which to build therapies. They could build this into a base editor, a prime editor or any number of unique combinations. I have not seen any in use published data for this system yet. Its still very early in its discovery phase of development.

Science

They have no targets selected for a pipeline yet.
