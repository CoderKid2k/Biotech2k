Sherlock Biosciences

Profile

They are solely focused on using CRISPR technology for Diagnostics. I find that exciting, but it probably has more limited potential. They have 2 platforms called SHERLOCK and INSPECTR.

Science

The SHERLOCK system works by using a guide RNA to find a target sequence in the DNA or RNA. Then the nuclease will cut and also cut the reporter molecules nearby. This gives a diagnostic read out.

The INSPECTR is a system that can be programmed to detect single nucletide differences between 2 strands of DNA or RNA. When the difference is detected, the reporter molecule is cut and give a read out.
