Pacific Biosciences

Profile

Pacific Bioscience is a sequencing company making machines for both long and short read sequencing. They are developing 2 machines with 1 for long reads and 1 for short reads. They deploy a technology called Single Molecule Real-Time (SMRT) Sequencing. This technology uses chips where they place the DNA into the well along with nucleotides that are tagged with fluorescence. There is a lens at the bottom of the well that will read the flashes of light for each base as it is copied. They run this DNA in a circular fashion so it makes multiple passes around the DNA. This allows them to build a consensus of the base for each nucleotide in the sequence. The software then builds the sequence using the consensus.

Science

Revio is their newest version of the long read sequencer. It has a lot of updates. It has the best accuracy of the long read sequencers. They can even read methylation patterns on the DNA using their sequencers. The Revio pushes the cost of sequencing to below $1,000 for long reads.

Onso is their new short read sequencer they are developing from their buy out of Omiome. The Onso is designed to be the most accurate short read sequencer. This technology uses Sequencing by Binding technology for short reads.
